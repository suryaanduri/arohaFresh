About Aroha Fresh

Aroha Fresh began with a simple promise: Pure • Fresh • Natural. On our 17‑acre farm, we raise cows and buffaloes with care, maintain clean sheds, and follow responsible farming. Our hens and flower plots enrich farm biodiversity and soil health.

Mission

Deliver uncompromised nutrition from transparent, traceable farms.

Promise

No adulterants. Chilled chain. Early‑morning delivery.

Farm at a glance

  • 🌾 17 acres — mixed fodder & flower plots
  • 🐄 Dairy herd — cows & buffaloes
  • 🐔 Poultry — hens for natural ecosystem balance
  • 🚚 Morning dispatch — doorstep by 6:00 AM
